About LifeLink

Blood Donation & Emergency Assistance Platform

🔴 About the Project

LifeLink is a web-based blood donation and emergency assistance platform designed to connect blood donors with patients during critical situations. The system enables users to quickly search for donors based on blood group and location.

The project focuses on reducing delays in emergency blood requirements by providing a fast, reliable, and easy-to-use interface. Registered donors can volunteer by submitting their details securely through the platform.

👩‍💻 About the Team

LifeLink is developed by a dedicated team of Computer Science students who are passionate about using technology for social impact. Each team member contributed to frontend design, backend integration, and system testing.

The team followed a collaborative and iterative development approach, ensuring usability, data security, and scalability. Academic guidance and continuous feedback helped refine the project into a reliable solution.

This project reflects the team’s commitment to learning modern web technologies while addressing real-world healthcare challenges.
